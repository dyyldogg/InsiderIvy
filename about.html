InsiderIvy - Who We Are

At Insider Ivy, we understand the challenges and expenses associated with the college application process. As former high school students ourselves, we faced the daunting task of applying to colleges without access to expensive tutors or consultants.

Instead, we relied on the generosity of older students we knew who shared their application experiences and insights. We realized the value of connecting current high school students with a network of current college students who can provide guidance and support.

Our mission is to make this invaluable resource accessible to all students. We believe that college consulting shouldn't be limited to those who can afford it. Through our platform, high school students can connect with college students who have successfully navigated the application process for the colleges they aspire to attend.

Not only does this provide affordable assistance to high school students, but it also offers college students an opportunity to share their knowledge, help others, and earn money.

"We believe in empowering students to achieve their college dreams by leveraging the experiences of those who have gone before them."
